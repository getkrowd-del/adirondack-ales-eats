Our Story

More Than a Brewpub.
It's a Way of Life.

We started with a dream, a copper kettle, and a deep love for the Adirondacks. Every beer we brew and every dish we serve is a tribute to this wild, beautiful corner of New York.

From our flagship ales to our seasonal farm-to-table small plates, everything at Adirondack Ales & Eats is crafted with intention — and poured with pride.
